Airline Passangers Satisfaction Survey Analysis

My client just got the latest passenger survey results and it looks like the satisfaction rate dipped under 50% for the first time ever. The leadership team needed to take action fast to bring customers satisfaction back. They wanted to identify the service areas where customer satisfaction get hit most. They also needed to know the passenger and flight segments where customers' dissatisfied most. The customer survey result was just one big CSV file generated from a third party application. I was fully in charge to do the data cleasing, transformation, data modeling, report development and setting up recommendations based on the analysis. The management took immidiate action and implemented different corrective measures based on the findings and recommendations made and able to achive 88% customer satisfaction rate within a one year period of time.
